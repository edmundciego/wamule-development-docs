# Financial responsibilities and payment recording

Finance/Admin staff are responsible for accurate payment entry, receipt records, proof review, bank-reference quality, payment-request status, and collection escalation. Sales staff should route received-payment evidence through the approved finance process.

## Cash payments

Verify customer and contract, amount received, transaction type, authorized staff member, receipt number, receipt date, issuer, and notes. Record the payment once, then confirm it appears in the ledger.

## Online transfers

Record the customer, contract where applicable, amount, transfer method, bank reference, and uploaded proof. Confirm through the approved company bank-review process before treating it as financially complete.

:::danger Required separation
A reservation is not a payment. Deposit readiness is not ledger confirmation. AI/smart insights cannot confirm financial truth. A payment must be separately recorded in the financial records.
:::
