# Receipt, bank-reference, proof, and correction standards

## Recording standards

- Use manual receipt numbers exactly as issued; do not reuse or invent a number.
- Capture bank references accurately for online transfers.
- Upload approved proof to the correct customer/transaction context.
- Review missing receipt, proof, and bank-reference queues daily.

## Correcting mistakes

First confirm whether a payment already saved. Preserve financial accountability: correct the authoritative record through the authorized workflow, record the reason, and verify the revised customer balance, request, and report. Do not use destructive purge tools for normal financial corrections.
