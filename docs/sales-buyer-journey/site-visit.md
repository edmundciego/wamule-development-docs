# Lot discussion and site visits

Before discussing a lot as available, review its current lot status and active reservation context. Record the buyer’s preferred lot and alternates; do not promise a lot based solely on a prior conversation.

When scheduling a site visit, record the date/time, location, visit type, assigned staff member, and preparation notes. Afterward mark the visit `completed`, `no_show`, `cancelled`, or `rescheduled`, then record the outcome and next follow-up.

:::warning A site visit is not a reservation
Scheduling or completing a visit does not hold a lot, accept a deposit, or create a payment.
:::
