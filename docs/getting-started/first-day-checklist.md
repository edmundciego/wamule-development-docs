# First-day checklist

- [ ] Sign in with your own account and confirm the displayed role.
- [ ] Review the Dashboard and identify where priority work appears.
- [ ] Open one training lead, customer, and lot record with a trainer.
- [ ] Learn how to add an activity and follow-up without changing financial data.
- [ ] Read the distinction between site visits, reservations, deposits, and payments.
- [ ] Review the support and feedback process.
- [ ] Confirm which work your role may approve, record, or only view.

Complete the [Start-of-day workflow](../daily-operations/start-of-day) with a trainer before working live records.
