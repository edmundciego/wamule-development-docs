# Signing in

Use the Wamule Operations Platform login page provided by Wamule Development. Enter only your own approved staff credentials.

1. Open the approved application address.
2. Select **Login** and enter your email and password.
3. Confirm that the protected sidebar and your permitted pages appear.
4. Select **Logout** when leaving a shared device.

:::warning Access problems
Do not share passwords or use another person’s account. If access is missing or incorrect, follow [Login and access problems](../troubleshooting-support/common-issues#login-and-access-problems).
:::

Your role determines what you can view or change. See [User roles](roles).
