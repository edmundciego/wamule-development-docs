# Post-sales checklists

Post-sales checklists coordinate the work after approval or contract start: agreement readiness, document readiness, payment setup, collections handoff, assignee, notes, and overall status.

Checklist state is operational readiness only. It does not create contracts, payments, schedule changes, document approvals, or automatic collections actions.

**Media:** Workflow diagram, annotated screenshot, checklist.
