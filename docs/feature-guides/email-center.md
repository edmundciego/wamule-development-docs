# Email Center

The Email Center is an Admin/Super Admin-controlled notification outbox. Authorized users can queue, preview, send, retry, cancel, and status-track supported notifications.

It is not a full inbox, reply-management tool, customer campaign system, or automatic email workflow. Verify recipient, subject, content, and status before sending or retrying.
