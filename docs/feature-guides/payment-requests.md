# Payment requests

Payment requests are expected-payment records with customer, optional contract, amount, due date, reason, notes, creator, and status: Draft, Sent, Paid, or Cancelled.

Keep the request status current, but do not mark it Paid merely because it was sent or a buyer says they paid. Confirm the corresponding ledger payment.
