# Leads

Leads is the sales workspace for public inquiries, applicant-linked prospects, stage management, ownership, lot interest, notes, site visits, follow-ups, and reservations.

Keep the stage truthful, assign an owner, and maintain the buyer’s next action. The Lead Smart Summary, where available, is advisory context only.

## Opening a lead from an alert

Dashboard and Daily Brief links can open a specific lead and focus the relevant work area. A follow-up link may include the lead, focus, and follow-up identifiers so the selected lead opens with the follow-up highlighted. Reservation, site-visit, and Smart Summary links use the same lead workspace with the appropriate focus.

If the lead or task no longer exists, the page should show a safe fallback and leave the normal list and filters available. Confirm the current lead stage and next action before creating a replacement task.

Operational Attention is source-backed. An overdue follow-up belongs to an existing lead, is still open, and has a due time earlier than the current Belize-time value. Completed or cancelled follow-ups are not counted.
