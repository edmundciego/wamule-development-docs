# Reports and CSV exports

Reports provide filtered operational views and client-side CSV exports for payments, balances, sales, follow-ups, visits, reservations, applications, post-sales, workload, demand, lots, and missing items.

Use filters before exporting; protect exported files as operational data. Report accuracy depends on the status and information recorded by staff.
