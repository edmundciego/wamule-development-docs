# Contracts

Contracts records the customer, lot, final purchase price, initial deposit, term, monthly payment, start date, due day, status, and signed-document path. Verify every commercial term before creation.

Voided, cancelled, and archived contracts remain historical records. Use the approved reason-based workflow; do not treat void/cancel as a way to erase financial or audit history.
