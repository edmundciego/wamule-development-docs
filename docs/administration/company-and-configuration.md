# Company and operational configuration

Authorized settings areas include Company Profile, Public Application Settings, Payment Methods, Installment Plans, Lot Sizes, Fee Types, Reservation Settings, AI Settings, and notification-related settings.

Before changing a setting, confirm the business decision, effective date, owner, and expected impact. Configuration changes should be reviewed as operational changes, not treated as casual data entry.
