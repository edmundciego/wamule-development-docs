# Privileged Super Admin controls

:::danger Super Admin procedure
Only Super Admin users should create/invite users, change roles, manage high-trust AI/configuration controls, or use Data Management. Verify the requester, scope, and business approval before acting.
:::

AI settings control availability of application reviews, Daily Brief, and collections assistance. They do not give AI authority to approve applications, reconcile payments, or alter business records.

Data Management is a controlled procedure for approved test or incorrect records. It requires explicit confirmation and is not routine correction for live customers, contracts, payments, or historical records.

**Media:** Text, checklist. Do not create public video walkthroughs for Data Management.
