# Client acceptance, training, and go-live checklists

## Client acceptance

- [ ] Test public application and inquiry flows using fictional data.
- [ ] Verify protected routes, role boundaries, and key workflows with test accounts.
- [ ] Confirm lots, plans, payment methods, fee types, and company profile.
- [ ] Validate applications, customer/contract flow, payments, proof, reports, collections, and post-sales.

## Staff training

- [ ] Train each role against its permitted workflows.
- [ ] Practice buyer journey and financial controls using training data.
- [ ] Verify support/feedback and escalation ownership.

## Go-live

- [ ] Remove or clearly mark test records under the approved process.
- [ ] Confirm production configuration and accountable owners.
- [ ] Complete launch authority and data-verification sign-off.
