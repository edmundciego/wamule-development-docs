# Weekly management review

Super Admins and Admins should review reports for sales pipeline movement, follow-ups, visits, reservations, applications, lots, outstanding balances, missing information, post-sales workload, and demand.

Confirm owners for recurring exceptions, review material data corrections in the Audit Trail, and identify documentation that needs updating after a policy or workflow change.

**Media:** Checklist, annotated screenshot, workflow diagram.
