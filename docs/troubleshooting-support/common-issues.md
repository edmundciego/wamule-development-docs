# Common issues and troubleshooting

## Login and access problems

Confirm the correct account, role, and application address. Do not share credentials. Report missing/incorrect access with your name, role, page, and safe description of the problem.

## Data, lots, reservations, and duplicates

For missing/incorrect customer information, incorrect lot status, reservation conflicts, or duplicate indicators: stop before creating a conflicting record; compare linked records; capture the record ID safely; and escalate through the approved data-correction path.

## Applications, payments, proof, and reports

Verify the underlying application/customer/payment/request record before assuming a summary is wrong. Missing receipt numbers or payment proof should be corrected with the authorized financial workflow. Report discrepancies with expected versus actual result, filters used, and safe reproduction steps.
